About The Law Office of William McElroy


 The Law Office of William McElroy is strictly devoted to your criminal law needs, from bail reduction to assault, DUI or drug charges. Because I specialize in criminal defense, I have a thorough understanding of Pennsylvania criminal law. I stay up to date with ongoing changes and can help with any modifications to current or past cases with regard to the newest laws.

 Your case is important to me. That is why I will work with you before, during and after your case to ensure that you are completely satisfied and to help you make plans for any necessary next steps. I am an experienced trial attorney and has successfully handled DUI, drug-related and criminal cases throughout Montgomery County and Bucks County, including homicide trials, “designer drug” and wiretap cases.
